Sunita Pandya Lyn Williams

Astronaut Sunita L. Williams, STS-116 mission specialist, participates in the mission's third planned session of extravehicular activity (EVA)

  • 1965 - Born in Euclid, Ohio
  • 1993 - Williams began training at the U.S. Naval Test Pilot School
  • 1995 - She received a Master of Science degree in Engineering Management from Florida Institute of Technology.
  • 1998 - Williams was deployed on Saipan when she was selected by NASA for the astronaut program
  • 2006 - Williams was launched to the International Space Station (ISS) with STS-116, aboard Space Shuttle Discovery to join the Expedition 14 crew
  • 2007 - On January 31, February 4, and February 9, 2007, she completed three spacewalks from the ISS
  • 2015 - NASA announced Williams as one of the first astronauts for U.S. Commercial spaceflights. Subsequently, she has started working with Boeing and SpaceX to train in their commercial crew vehicles, along with other chosen astronauts
